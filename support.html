ItKnows – Support

Welcome to the support page for ItKnows, an adaptive endurance training app for runners. ItKnows connects to a Bluetooth heart-rate monitor, tracks your run data, and helps guide your personal aerobic training load in real time.

FAQ – Quick Answers

  • My heart-rate monitor will not connect.
    Make sure your monitor is awake, worn correctly, and close to your phone. Try opening the monitor connection screen again and rescanning. If your monitor is already connected elsewhere, disconnect it from the other app or device first.
  • Why does the app request Location permission?
    ItKnows uses location and movement data during runs to measure speed and related run signals. On some devices and operating systems, location permission may also be required for Bluetooth scanning or connection features to work correctly.
  • Why does the app need Bluetooth permission?
    Bluetooth permission is required so the app can scan for, connect to, and reconnect to your compatible heart-rate monitor.
  • Why is vibration or haptic feedback not working?
    Check that your device supports vibration, that haptics are enabled in system settings, and that your phone is not limiting vibration behaviour.
  • My subscription did not unlock premium features.
    Tap Restore purchases on the subscription screen if you already have a valid App Store subscription. If that does not work, contact support and include the device model and a short description of the issue.
  • How does the first run work?
    Your first run is used to calibrate the app to your physiology. It is important that this first run matches the distance of your typical daily run and is done at your usual training pace. After that, ItKnows uses that calibration to guide your personal aerobic training load in future runs.
  • Does ItKnows replace speedwork?
    No. ItKnows is designed to build endurance and guide aerobic training load. It is the foundation of your training and helps support speedwork, not replace it.
  • Will the app remember my monitor?
    Yes. Once connected, ItKnows is designed to remember your preferred heart-rate monitor and reconnect to it automatically when possible.
